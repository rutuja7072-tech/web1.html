Maharashtra is the state in India. It is famous for its forts, festivals, Lavani dance, brave history of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, and modern cities like Mumbai and Pune.It is a beautiful state in India known for its rich culture, delicious food like Puran Poli, best academy of defence forces and many more. It shows a perfect mix of tradition, nature, and modern life.
